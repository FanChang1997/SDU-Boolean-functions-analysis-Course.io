Topics in Combinatorics / Analysis of Boolean Functions (2025春季讨论班：布尔函数分析学)

    Welcome! This is a reading group organized by School of Mathematics, Shandong University . In this reading group, we will mostly be studying Boolean functions over the hypercube. Our primary tools come from discrete Fourier analysis and discrete Probability. We mainly focus on the following topics:

  • Fundamentals of Boolean function analysis (including hypercontractive inequality, the KKL theorem, Junta theorems by Friedgut and Bourgain, and the invariance principle,...)

  • Applications in Extremal Combinatorics (including EKR-type problems, Expanded hypergraph Turán problems, Junta approximation method, isoperimetric influence-based method,...)

  • Applications in Probabilistic Combinatorics (including Concentration of measure, Bernoulli Percolation, Khintchine inequality and convex bodies, Threshold phenomena for random discrete structures,...)

  • Applications in TCS (including Property testing, Learning theory, Hardness of approximation,...)
